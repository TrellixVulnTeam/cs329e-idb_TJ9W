Brandon Sanderson

Brandon Sanderson is an American fantasy and science fiction writer. He is best known for his Mistborn series and his work in finishing Robert Jordan's epic fantasy series The Wheel of Time.

Born: December 19, 1975

Nationality: American

Alma Mater: Brigham Young University (B.A., M.A.)
